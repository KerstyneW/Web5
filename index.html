Prince Edward County Tourist App

Our Ideal

Tourists visiting Prince Edward County (PEC) will be able to access a web app that displays an interactive map with locations of interest and pop-up information about these locations. The app will also have general information about PEC and it's history.

The Reality

The current maps intended for tourists detailing locations of interest in PEC are either out of date, not interactive, or bad quality.

The Consequences

There is currently no easy to use, up to date application that PEC tourists can access to view locations of interest and easily plan their visits.

Our Proposal

Implement a web app to display locations of interest in Prince Edward County (visitor information centers, wineries, breweries, cideries, parks, trails). The app should be interactive (pop-up info for each displayed location) and also contain additional information on Prince Edward County, it's general history, and other interesting/noteworthy information.
